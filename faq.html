Evaluations are $275. 30-minute sessions are $85. 60-minute sessions are $150. The initial 15-minute consultation is free. Sliding-scale options available — ask on your call.
We don't currently bill AHCCCS or DDD directly. If your child has a funding source, please mention it on your call — we can often point you toward an in-network provider who's a great fit.
Approach & philosophy
We believe different brains belong. Our goal is communication that fits your child — not making your child look or sound a certain way. We don't try to eliminate stimming, scripting, or autistic traits. We build functional communication around who your child already is.
Some children learn language in whole chunks (scripts or echolalia) before breaking them into single words — a style called gestalt language processing. It's a valid path to language. We follow the natural stages instead of pushing single-word imitation.

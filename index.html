When to seek help

Trust your gut — earlier is always easier.

If any of these feel familiar, a free consultation is a good next step. You don't need a diagnosis or a referral to reach out.

Few or late first words

Toddlers who use gestures more than words, or haven't hit typical milestones.

Hard to understand

Familiar adults or peers miss words, leaving your child frustrated.

Stuttering or blocks

Repetitions, blocks, or tension that's starting to affect confidence.

Scripting or echolalia

Natural-language support for gestalt language processors.

Needs AAC

From trial and device selection to daily use at school and home.

Feeding challenges

Sensory-informed feeding and mealtime coaching for the whole family.
